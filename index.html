This training is designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills to navigate and utilise the K1 CRM system (known in the market as QContact). By mastering the system, you'll be able to maintain accurate records, and manage communication efficiently across all channels.

About QContact

QContact is a comprehensive communication platform that unifies calls, emails, live chat, WhatsApp, social media, and more into a single interface. It ensures that all customer interactions are recorded systematically and managed efficiently, providing a seamless experience for both agents and customers.

Core Purposes of the K1 System

The K1 CRM is a key tool that supports three main functions:

  • Communication: Organising all client and internal interactions in one place.
  • Record-Keeping: Maintaining accurate, up-to-date information for quick access and reference.
  • Managing Queries: Tracking, prioritising, and resolving client queries efficiently.

Merge Contacts

You can merge duplicate contacts using either the Contacts list view or directly from a Contact Card.

  • Option 1: From Contacts List
    1. Click the Contacts button to open the list/column view.
    2. Click the Merge button in the top-right corner.
    3. Search for the primary contact using any of the following:
      • ID Number
      • First Name
      • Last Name
      • Contact Number
      • Email Address
      • Membership Number
      • Payroll Number
    4. Search for the secondary contact using the same criteria.
    5. Select the information to merge.
    6. Click Save to complete or Cancel to abort.
  • Option 2: From Contact Card
    1. Open the Contact Card of the primary contact.
    2. Click Merge Contact.
    3. Search for the secondary contact using the criteria above.
    4. Select the information to merge.
    5. Click Save to complete or Cancel to abort.

Call Handling: Existing Contact Card & Case

Manage calls by selecting existing contacts and linking existing conversations to cases.

  • Answer or Make a Call

    • Accept an incoming call or initiate a call from the desired contact.

  • Re-Assign a Call:
    1. Click the Re-Assign button.
    2. Search for the correct contact by ID, name, number, email, membership, or payroll.
    3. Select the required contact card.
  • Link Conversation to a Case:
    1. Click the Link Case Button
    2. Search for the case by typing details or scrolling through the list.
    3. Select the required case.

Call Handling: New Contact Card & Case

Manage calls by creating new contacts and linking conversations to cases.

  • Answer or Make a Call

    • Accept an incoming call or initiate a call from the desired contact.

  • Re-Assign a Call:
    1. Click the Re-Assign button.
    2. Search for the correct contact by ID, name, number, email, membership, or payroll.
    3. If the contact does not exist, click Add New.
    4. Complete the member details to create the contact.
  • Link Conversation to a Case:
    1. Click the Link Case Button
    2. Search for the case by typing details or scrolling through the list.
    3. To create a new case, click Add Case.
    4. Complete the case details to finalise the process.

Call Handling: Transfering a call

Transfer a call to another user efficiently, either immediately or after consultation.

  1. Click the Transfer button.
  2. Choose User as the transfer type.
  3. Search for the desired user by typing their name or scrolling through the list.
  4. Select a transfer method:
    • Blind Transfer : Ends your call immediately, rings the new user.
    • Warm Transfer : Keeps you on the line until the new user answers.
  5. Click Complete Transfer.

Transfer a Live Call

Answer an Email

Efficiently pick up and respond to emails assigned to your department.

  1. Click the Users button.
  2. Select the Departments tab.
  3. Click the dropdown next to the department containing your name.
  4. Click the Pick Up button next to the email you want to answer.
  5. Click the green Answer button once the email is offered to you.
